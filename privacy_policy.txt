
Asset Tracker – Privacy Policy & Account Deletion
Last updated: 21 December 2025
1. Introduction
Asset Tracker is a personal finance and asset management app developed by S One Connection.
This Privacy Policy explains:
What data we collect
How backup & restore work
How you can delete your account and all related data
How to contact us
If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.
2. What data we collect
When you use Asset Tracker, we may collect the following information:
2.1 Account data
Email address (used to create and log in to your Asset Tracker account)
Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication.
We do not see, store, or manage your raw password.
2.2 In-app data you provide
Asset names, categories, and amounts
Optional notes created inside the app
These data are used only to provide core app functionality and backup/restore features.
2.3 Automatic data from third-party services (Ads)
Asset Tracker uses Google AdMob to show ads.
AdMob may collect:
Device identifiers
IP address
Ad interaction and usage data
This data is collected and processed by Google according to their own privacy policy.
We do not sell your personal data to anyone.
3. How we use your data
Your data are used only for:
Creating and securing your Asset Tracker account
Saving your assets and notes
Backing up and restoring your data
Showing ads (via Google AdMob) to keep the app free
We do not use your data for unrelated purposes.
4. Data storage, backup and restore
Asset Tracker uses Firebase services (such as Firebase Authentication and Firebase databases) to store account information and backups securely.
Backups are linked to your email address
Data can be restored after reinstalling the app or changing devices
Communication between the app and Firebase is protected by industry-standard security measures
If you reinstall the app or change devices, you can sign in with the same email address and use the Restore data option inside the app.
5. Data retention
Your data remain stored as long as your Asset Tracker account exists
If you stop using the app without deleting your account, backups may remain stored
When you request account deletion, your account and backups are deleted within 30 days, unless we are legally required to keep certain data longer
6. Your rights
You can:
View your data inside the Asset Tracker app
Create backups and restore them
Request permanent deletion of your account and backups
Contact us with any privacy or data-related questions
7. Account & data deletion
You can permanently delete your Asset Tracker account and all related backups.
Important: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
7.1 Delete from inside the app
Open Asset Tracker and log in
Tap the three-line (☰) menu in the top-right corner
Select Delete account
Read the warning message carefully
Tap Continue to confirm or Cancel to abort
After confirmation, your account and backups will be permanently deleted.
7.2 Request deletion by email
If you cannot access the app:
Send an email to support@s1connection.com
Subject: “Asset Tracker – Account deletion request”
Send the email from the same address used to log in
Confirm that you understand deletion is permanent
We will process the request within 30 days and send confirmation.
8. Children’s privacy
Asset Tracker is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it.
9. Changes to this policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.
10. Contact
If you have questions about privacy, data safety, or account deletion, contact:
S One Connection
Email: support@s1connection.com
